In the creation of this website, my primary goal was to establish a platform that would highlight the significant contributions of women word artists in the literary art world. It was my belief that the work of women writers has not always received the recognition and appreciation that it deserves, despite the fact that women have made substantial contributions to the field of literature throughout history. By creating a space that focuses exclusively on the work of women word artists, my aim was to help raise awareness of their contributions and provide a platform that would allow their voices to be heard. Through this website, I hope to promote and recognize the important role that women have played and continue to play in shaping the literary landscape, both in the past and in the present. Furthermore, my goal in creating this website was to serve as a collection of work that amplifies the voices of women artists who have traditionally been marginalized in the art world. I believe that focusing on women word artists is a crucial step towards creating a more inclusive and equitable literary art landscape, and that this website could play an important role in helping to achieve that goal. By providing a platform for both established and emerging women writers, this website could help to showcase the immense talent and creativity of women artists, and encourage more women to pursue careers in the literary arts. Through the promotion of the work of women word artists, I hope to contribute to a more diverse and inclusive literary art world that values and celebrates the contributions of all artists, regardless of gender or background.
