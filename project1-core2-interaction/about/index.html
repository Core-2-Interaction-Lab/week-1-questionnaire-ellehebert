BACK TO THE BEGINNING
ABOUT
DESIGNED BY Elle Hebert - Parsons school of design - Core 2 Interaction - Spring 2023
“On Beginnings” From Madness, Rack and Honey by Mary Ruefle. The beginnings and ends of poetry. Fonts Used: Trade Bee Four & Trade Gothic Next LT Pro Condensed. Color Codes (In order of pages, text/background): #B6634A/D4B996, #ABC5DE/ABC5DE, #E7CDCB/6B4646, #D6D2E8/416D60, #E9A892/B25A35, #BAC5A5/715657. #E1D6BA/4D6676. The story was design with the intent to take the viewer on a poetic journey through type and color experimentation, with emphasis on editorial design.
“In life, the number of beginnings is exactly equal to the number of endings: no one has yet to begin a life who will not end it.”
