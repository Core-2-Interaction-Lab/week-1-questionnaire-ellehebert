Spring Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

Sour Dough Bread Truffle Aioli Turkey Deli Slices Bacon Swiss Cheese Lettuce Balsalmic Vinaigrette Salt and Pepper

Instructions

1. Cook bacon in a pan/skillet for 5-6 minutes.
2. Chop lettuce into a shred.
3. Spread truffle aioli onto one side of each piece of bread.
4. Assemble sandwhich in order: cheese, turkey, bacon, lettuce.
5. Drizzle the balsalmic vinaigrette and sprinkle the salt and pepper onto the sandwhich.
6. Cut sandwich in half and serve.

Happy Spring!
